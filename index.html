Pacific Rim

Pacific Rim is the best movie that has ever been made in our life time.
The storyline of the show is what I consider peak fiction. The amount of action
between the kaijus and the jeagers is incredible. Character development
is perfect for the story. The tradedy is very realistic in correlation to the storyline.
This is the best movie ever.

Now You See Me

Pacific rim is definitly not the best movie that has ever benn made. For example Now You See Me
stomps Pacific Rim in the ground based on storyline alone. The individual acting from everyone is
many levels higher than the acting in Pacific Rim. Now You See Me also has a better way of
grabbing and holding onto the viewers attention since the pace of the story is consistently interesting.
Each performance by the magicians unique and breathtaking. Overall way better than Pacific Rim.
